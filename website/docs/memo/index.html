The Contract Conversion
The problem: T&M contracts pay by the hour. We need outcome-based pricing.
This work is non-personal services. We work with the CO to convert it to:
A  →  Firm-Fixed-Price
B  →  Fixed Price Incentive (FAR 16.204)
C  →  Performance-Based Outcomes (FAR 37.6)
D  →  Minimize labor. Push costs to ODCs.
       If they don't pay — dispute with GAO.
A non-personal services dispute is public. Public performance proof defeats cost/price realism challenges on every future bid. We win the case, or we win the press. Either way, we win.
